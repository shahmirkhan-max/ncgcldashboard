BIPL ‚Äì Smallholder Agri Input Finance (6-Month Cycle)
Action: Approve 10% Second-Loss Pilot
Product Type
Seasonal smallholder agri input portfolio (Islamic structure)
Guarantee Cover
10% NCGCL second-loss (over 10% SBP first-loss)
NCGCL Exposure
PKR 100m cap on PKR 1.0bn portfolio
Facility Size
PKR 1.0bn
Revolving portfolio to finance fertilizer, seed and other inputs for smallholders; self-liquidating at harvest.
Guarantee Structure
SBP 10% first-loss, followed by NCGCL 10% second-loss, with BIPL retaining the remaining 80% ultimate loss. Claims on NCGCL layer only after SBP layer is fully utilized and losses are validated.
Tenor & Pricing
6-month bullet loans aligned with crop cycle; guarantee fee of 1.0% flat, charged at maturity in line with approved pricing grid and Shariah-compliant structure.
Eligibility & Portfolio Criteria
  • Small farmers with landholding up to 12.5 acres, verified CNIC and KYC completed.
  • Internal and ECIB checks; no severe delinquency history.
  • Portfolio diversification across crop types and regions; caps on single-borrower and group exposure.
Controls, Reporting & Claims
  • Mandatory Takaful; standardized loan documentation and digital MIS.
  • Monthly reporting on disbursements, PAR and recoveries; joint quarterly portfolio reviews.
  • NCGCL claims admissible only once SBP 10% first-loss is fully absorbed, PAR thresholds tested, and recovery efforts documented.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Weather and yield risk: short tenor, geographic diversification, and insurance.
  • Moral hazard: high residual exposure for BIPL; hard PAR trip-wires and RAC compliance.
  • Operational risk: standardized processes, audit rights and independent monitoring.
Decision Sought: Approve PKR 1.0bn BIPL smallholder agri input portfolio with NCGCL providing 10% second-loss cover (PKR 100m maximum exposure), over SBP‚Äôs 10% first-loss, subject to adherence to agreed RAC, PAR triggers and reporting standards.
BIPL ‚Äì Long-Term Agri & Productive Asset Finance
Action: Approve 15% First-Loss PCG (Pilot at 10%)
Product Type
Long-tenor agri & productive asset portfolio (mechanisation, solar, storage, livestock)
Guarantee Cover
Up to 15% NCGCL first-loss (pilot launch at 10% FL)
NCGCL Exposure
Capped at PKR 100m across ~PKR 660m portfolio
Indicative Portfolio Size
~PKR 660m*
Portfolio of term loans for farm mechanization, solar irrigation, on-farm storage, dairy/livestock and other cashflow-generating assets. *Ceiling and utilization to be phased with pilot performance.
Guarantee Structure
NCGCL provides first-loss protection on the underlying portfolio up to 15% of outstanding (capped at PKR 100m), with the pilot launching at a 10% first-loss level and ramping up subject to performance and reviews.
Tenor & Pricing
Tenors up to 7‚Äì8 years, with quarterly or semi-annual amortizing instalments. Guarantee fee of 1.0% p.a. on guaranteed outstanding exposure, in line with the approved MAGFA-linked pricing grid.
Eligibility & Risk Criteria
  • Borrowers with demonstrable and verifiable cashflows to service term debt.
  • Compliance with both BIPL‚Äôs long-term credit policy and NCGCL RAC, including sector caps.
  • Hard PAR threshold (e.g. 10%) at portfolio level triggering review and potential halt of further utilization.
Security & Safeguards
  • e-Passbook where applicable, and charge over financed assets and receivables.
  • Mandatory insurance/Takaful on financed assets; standard documentation and valuation norms.
  • NCGCL retains right to pause utilization where covenants or triggers are breached.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Long-term asset and price risk mitigated via rigorous appraisal, stress testing and phased deployment.
  • NCGCL exposure is capped at PKR 100m and structured as first-loss to crowd-in BIPL capital.
  • Data and learning from pilot phase to inform scale-up and any recalibration of terms.
Decision Sought: Approve BIPL long-term agri & asset PCG structure with NCGCL first-loss cover up to 15% (pilot launch at 10%), capped at PKR 100m exposure, with phased deployment and strict RAC adherence.
PMIC ‚Äì Portfolio Credit Guarantee for Microfinance Liquidity
Action: Approve 50% Pari Passu PCG
Product Type
Portfolio credit guarantee on wholesale funding to MFIs/MFBs
Guarantee Cover
50% NCGCL pari passu on principal
NCGCL Exposure
Up to PKR 1.0bn on PKR 2.0bn facility
Facility Size
PKR 2.0bn
Facility extended to PMIC to on-lend to eligible MFIs/MFBs, channeled to women, micro and climate-linked segments as per agreed RAC.
Guarantee Structure
NCGCL guarantees 50% of principal losses on a pari passu basis with PMIC, up to an aggregate cap of PKR 1.0bn. Losses and recoveries are shared proportionately between PMIC and NCGCL.
Tenor & Pricing
Medium-term facility (up to 5 years). Guarantee fee charged as per approved grid, linked to underlying risk profile and portfolio performance.
Use of Proceeds
On-lending to MFIs/MFBs for eligible microfinance products with clear onward lending criteria, exclusions and pro-poor focus.
Eligibility & Covenants
  • MFIs/MFBs must meet minimum rating, governance and performance thresholds defined in RAC.
  • Portfolio PAR kept within agreed limits (e.g. PAR30 / PAR90 thresholds).
  • Capital adequacy and leverage limits to be observed; breach triggers enhanced monitoring or suspension.
Security, Reporting & Claims
  • Assignment of receivables and pari passu charge/escrow arrangements where applicable.
  • Quarterly reporting pack with detailed portfolio performance, covenant compliance and early warning signals.
  • Standardized claims protocol; recoveries shared pro-rata between PMIC and NCGCL after claim payout.
Key Risks & Mitigants
  • Sectoral stress mitigated by diversified wholesale portfolio and strong apex institution.
  • Robust governance, covenants and NCGCL‚Äôs ability to suspend cover upon covenant breach.
  • Data-driven monitoring and regular joint review meetings.
Decision Sought: Approve PKR 2.0bn PMIC facility with 50% pari passu NCGCL guarantee on principal (maximum exposure PKR 1.0bn), subject to covenant-based monitoring, RAC compliance and right to suspend.
UNIDO / PBIC / SAFCO / TMF ‚Äì Sindh Consortium Pilot
Action: Approve Blended PCG Pilot
Product Type
Blended finance PCG for micro & small enterprises in Sindh
Guarantee Cover
40% NCGCL portfolio guarantee
NCGCL Exposure
PKR 80m cap on PKR 200m consortium facility
Facility Size
PKR 200m
Consortium facility combining UNIDO grant support, PBIC lines and MFI lending to micro & small enterprises in targeted Sindh districts.
Guarantee Structure
NCGCL provides a 40% portfolio guarantee over eligible loans, with total NCGCL exposure capped at PKR 80m. Grant support from UNIDO de-risks the underlying projects further, alongside consortium contributions.
Target Segments & Districts
Micro and small enterprises, with emphasis on women and youth-led businesses, in districts such as Tharparkar, Thatta, Sujawal, Badin and Larkana, aligned with UNIDO‚Äôs programme objectives.
Design & Governance
  • Clear roles for UNIDO, PBIC, SAFCO, TMF and NCGCL under a consortium framework agreement.
  • Eligibility and exclusion lists anchored in RAC and UNIDO‚Äôs project guidelines.
  • Quarterly consortium review meetings to track KPIs, impact and risk metrics.
Risk Management
  • Group- and community-based lending methodologies and social collateral where appropriate.
  • Portfolio PAR triggers with ability to rebalance, tighten criteria or pause disbursements.
  • NCGCL exposure ring-fenced to a defined pilot pool, with explicit exit and scale-up criteria.
Claims & Recovery
  • Standardized claim documentation verifying eligibility, procedures and loss realization.
  • Recoveries shared according to an agreed waterfall between PFIs, UNIDO-related funds (where applicable) and NCGCL.
Decision Sought: Approve PKR 200m UNIDO Sindh Consortium blended pilot with 40% NCGCL cover (PKR 80m cap), subject to signed consortium agreement, RAC adherence, KPIs and defined exit/scale-up triggers.
